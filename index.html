Malcom X

A man with a "Little" name and a BIG personality !

Biography

  • 1925 - Born in Omaha, NE
  • 1930's - Malcolm X became a ward of the court
  • 1958 - Married Betty Shabazz
  • 1960's - Civil rights leader
  • 1964 - Martin Luther King and Malcolm X met for the first time
  • 1965 - Died February 21, 1965 at 39 years old

Religion

After Malcom's mother suffered a mental breakdown, she was institionalized and her 9 children were split up amoung foster families. Though Malcolm did well in Junior High, Malcolm lost much interestin school and moved to Boston where he ran the streets and committed crimes. Just at the tender age of 21, Malcolm was arrested and sent to 10 years in prison. While in prison, Malcolm's brother came to visit and told him of his conversion to Islam. Malcolm started to study more about the teachings of Elijah Muhammad, Nation of Islam (NOI). Thus Malcolm X's conversion started to take shape. While in prision Malcolm considered his last name (Little) a slave name and choose to the letter X as his preferred name. His new muslim name became el-Hajj Malik el-Shabazz. Malcolm X became a powerful speaker for the Nation of Islam (NOI) during the 50's and 60's.
